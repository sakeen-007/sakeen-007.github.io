About Us

Founded in the Victorian era, our clothing brand has a rich heritage spanning over a century. With a legacy rooted in craftsmanship and timeless design, we have consistently evolved to meet the changing tastes of each generation while maintaining the elegance and quality that defined us from the start. Our commitment to excellence and tradition continues to inspire every piece we create, blending classic style with modern innovation for discerning customers around the world.

Today, with over 150 stores worldwide, we continue to serve our global clientele, from London to New York, Tokyo, and beyond. Our boutiques are designed to provide a personalized shopping experience, where customers can explore our carefully curated collections in an atmosphere of refined elegance. Whether you're looking for timeless staples or seasonal statement pieces, our brand remains a trusted name in fashion, offering premium clothing that stands the test of time.
